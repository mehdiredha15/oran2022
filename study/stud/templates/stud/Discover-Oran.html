Oran Story

Oran, also called ''the radiant one'', is located in the north-west of Algeria, 432km from the capital Algiers. It is famous for its historic places. Wahran (Arabic nickname) has known a succession of Arab-Berber dynasties. Arab-Berber dynasties. During the French colonization, it knew a fast growth, and becomes the second city of Algeria. After the independence, it remained the economic capital of the West of the country and the financial, commercial and industrial center.
